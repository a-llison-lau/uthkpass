About Us
關於我們 About Us
As a society built upon the identity of Hong Kong, we strived to promote interest in the social, political and economic affairs of Hong Kong. There are five main goals for UTHKPASS:
  • To conduct policy research on Hong Kong policies in order to fabricate innovating ideas on Hong Kong public affairs;
  • To educate the Hong Kong community at the University of Toronto on Hong Kong public affairs that is frequently raised in Hong Kong society;
  • To foster social awareness regarding Hong Kong minorities and marginalized groups to nurture an inclusive approach on the social issues;
  • To reinforce the Hong Kong community on the school campus so as to strengthen the connections amongst Hong Kong students;
  • To facilitate critical civic discourses regarding Hong Kong affairs through various forms of media.
